Hyderabad

Hyderabad is the largest city and capital of the state of Telangana. At over 12.2 million, it has the largest population of any city in the state Hyderabad is an A-1 city under the terms of development priorities. It is also known as city of pearls or city of Nizams.

Top sights in Hyderabad

  • Charminar
  • Golconda Fort
  • Birla Mandir
  • Salar Jung Museum
