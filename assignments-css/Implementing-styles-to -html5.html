  • Early Life: Born in Argentina in 1928, Che Guevara trained as a doctor but became a key figure in the Cuban Revolution.
  • Revolutionary Ideals: He believed in Marxist-Leninist ideology and fought for social justice, equality, and against imperialism.

AR Rahman

A.R. Rahman, whose original name was A.S. Dileep Kumar, was the son of a Tamil film composer. He learned to play piano as a young child growing up in Madras (now Chennai).

  • Awards and Honors: Rahman has received numerous awards and honors throughout his career, including several Filmfare Awards, National Film Awards, Grammy Awards, and the Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian award.
